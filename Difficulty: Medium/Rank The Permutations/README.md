<h2><a href="https://www.geeksforgeeks.org/problems/rank-the-permutations2229/1">Rank The Permutations</a></h2><h3>Difficulty Level : Difficulty: Medium</h3><hr><div class="problems_problem_content__Xm_eO" style="--text-color: var(--problem-text-color);"><p><span style="font-size: 18px;">Given a string&nbsp;<strong>s&nbsp;</strong>consisting of<strong>&nbsp;distinct</strong>&nbsp;lowercase characters, determine its rank among all its permutations when those are arranged in lexicographically sorted order.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 14pt;"><strong>Note:&nbsp;</strong>The&nbsp;<strong>rank</strong>&nbsp;of a string is its position (starting from 1) in the sorted list of all its permutations.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 18px;"><strong>Examples:</strong></span></p>
<pre><span style="font-size: 18px;"><strong>Input: </strong>s = "abc"</span>
<span style="font-size: 18px;"><strong>Output: </strong>1</span>
<strong><span style="font-size: 18px;">Explanation:</span> </strong><span style="font-size: 18px;">All permutations of "abc" arranged in lexicographical order are: [<span style="text-decoration: underline;">abc</span>, acb, bac, bca, cab, cba]. Since "abc" appears first in this list, its rank is 1.
</span></pre>
<pre><span style="font-size: 18px;"><strong>Input: </strong>s = "cba"<br></span><span style="font-size: 18px;"><strong>Output: </strong>6<br></span><span style="font-size: 14pt;"><strong style="font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Segoe UI', Roboto, Oxygen, Ubuntu, Cantarell, 'Open Sans', 'Helvetica Neue', sans-serif;">Explanation:</strong> All permutations of "cba" arranged in lexicographical order are: [abc<span style="font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Segoe UI', Roboto, Oxygen, Ubuntu, Cantarell, 'Open Sans', 'Helvetica Neue', sans-serif;">, acb, bac, bca, cab, <span style="text-decoration: underline;">cba</span>]</span>. Since "cba" appears at the 6th position in this list, its rank is 6.</span></pre>
<pre><span style="font-size: 18px;"><strong>Input: </strong>s = "string"<br></span><span style="font-size: 18px;"><strong>Output: </strong>598<br></span><span style="font-size: 14pt;"><strong style="font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Segoe UI', Roboto, Oxygen, Ubuntu, Cantarell, 'Open Sans', 'Helvetica Neue', sans-serif;">Explanation: </strong>If all permutations of "string" are arranged in lexicographical order, then "string" appears at the 598th position in the list. Therefore, its rank is 598.</span></pre>
